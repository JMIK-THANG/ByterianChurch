Apostle-pawl thurin

  • Pathian, Pa, Ziangkim tithei, lei le van semsuahtu cu ka pawm,
  • A Fapa neihsun Jesuh Krih, kan Bawipa cu ka pawm,
  • Anih cu Thlarai Thianghlim thawngin Fala hnahkhat Mari’n a vawn in a hring;
  • Pontius Pilate cun nasa zetin a hrem, khenbehin a um, a thi ih, an phum;
  • Mithi khua-ah a vung feh,
  • Ni thum ni-ah mithi lak ihsin a thosal; cuisin vanah a kai so;
  • Pathian, Pa, Ziangkim tithei vawrhlam kapah a hung to,
  • Cutawk hmun ihsin mithi le minung thu thensak dingin a ra sal ding;
  • Thlarau Thianghlim ka pawm,
  • Kawhhran Thianghlim le Huapkim ka pawm,
  • Mi thianghlimpawl pawlkawmnak ka pawm,
  • Sual ngaidamnak khal ka pawm,
  • Taksa thawhsalnak ka pawm,
  • Kumkhua nunnak thawn.

Nicea thurin

  • Ziangkim ti thei Pa Pathian,
  • Lei le van,hmuh theih le hmuh theih lo thil hmuahhmuah sersiamtu cu ka zum.
  • Bawipa pakhat Jesuh Khrih,
  • Pathian Fapa neihsun,
  • Leitlun hmuahhmuah um hlanih Pathian ih hrin mi cu ka zum.
  • Anih cu Pathian ihta Pathian, Tleunak ihtal Tleunak,
  • Pathian tak ihta Pathian tak, hrin mi, sersiam mi si lo,
  • Pa thawn sinak hmunkhat,
  • Thil hmuahhmuah hi Amah ihsi sersiam,
  • Amah cu kannih minung hrangah le kan Rundamnak si dingin,
  • Van ihsin a rung ṭum ih,
  • Thlarau Thianghlim hnaṭuannak in,
  • Fala thianghlim Mari ah cun tisa ah a rung cang ih, minung a rung si.
  • Pontia Pilat ih thu in kan hrangah khenbet in a um,
  • A tuar ih cun phum in a um.
  • Ni thumnakni ah Ca thianghlim ih rel vekin a thosal.
  • Van ah a kai ih, Pai vorh lamah a to.
  • Mithi le minung thuṭhen dingah, sunglawi zetin a rasal ding.
  • A ram cun cem ni a nei lo ding.
  • Thlarau Thianghlim, nunnak petu le Bawipa cu ka zum.
  • Amah cu Pa le Fapa ihsi suak, Pa le Fapa thawi bangrep ih biak le thangṭhat mi,
  • Profet-pawl hmangih thusimtu a si.
  • Apostol-pawl ih dinsuah mi zate huapṭheh Kawhhran thianghlim hi ka zum.
  • Sual ngaithiamnak dingih Baptisma pakhat hi ka pom.
  • Mithi thawhsalnak le a ra lai ding mi khawvel nunnak cu ka thlir. Amen.
